Eligibility Criteria

Ensure you meet the following requirements.

  • Candidates should have passed Higher Secondary Examination (10+2).
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are compulsory subjects.
  • Admission through counselling or management quota as per norms.
  • Original mark sheets and certificates must be produced during admission.
  • Community certificate and transfer certificate should be submitted.
  • Students awaiting results may apply provisionally.
